Blueberry Muffin

Description

This blueberry muffin recipe makes eight extra large and yummy breakfast.

I usually double the recipe and fill the muffin cups just to the top for a wonderful, extra-generously-sized, deli-style muffin.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose fluor
  • 3/4 cup white sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/3 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 large egg
  • 1/3 cup milk, or more as needed
  • 1 cup fresh blueberries

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F or 200 degrees C. Grease 8 muffin cups or line with paper liners.
  2. Whisk fluor, sugar, baking powder and salt together in a large bowl.
  3. Pour oil into a small liquid measuring cup. Add egg and enough milk to reach the 1-cup mark; stir until combined. Pour into flour mixture and mix just until batter is combined. Fold in blueberries; set batter aside.
  4. Spoon batter into the prepared muffin cups, filling right to the top.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted in the center of a muffin comes out clean, 20 to 25 minutes.
